About The Dosha Doctor

Our mission is to provide personalized holistic wellness solutions based on ancient wisdom and modern science.

Integrating Traditional Medicine with Modern Science

The Dosha Doctor was founded in 2010 with the vision of making holistic healthcare accessible to everyone. We combine the ancient wisdom of Ayurveda, Unani, and other traditional medicine systems with modern scientific research to provide effective wellness solutions.

Our team of certified doctors and wellness experts carefully curates each product in our inventory to ensure the highest quality and efficacy. We believe in treating the root cause of health issues rather than just the symptoms.

Whether you're looking for preventive care, chronic condition management, or general wellness support, The Dosha Doctor has solutions tailored to your unique constitution and health needs.
